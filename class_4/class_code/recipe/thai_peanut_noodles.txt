Quick Thai Peanut Noodles

⅓ cup Peanut Butter
⅓ cup soy sauce
2 teaspoons sesame oil
1 cup chicken broth
2 teaspoon minced garlic
2 teaspoon ginger (fresh or powder)
2 teaspoon Sriracha chili sauce
16 oz linguine noodles
sesame seeds for garnish
green onions for garnish

INSTRUCTIONS
Cook the noodles in a large pot according to package directions.
While the noodles are cooking, in a medium sauce pan whisk together peanut butter, soy sauce, sesame oil, chicken broth, minced garlic, ginger, and sriracha chili sauce. Cook over medium heat until smooth. Remove from the heat and set aside.
Drain the noodles and toss in the peanut sesame sauce. Serve immediately.
